What is the incircle of a triangle?
The incircle of a triangle is the biggest circle which can fit into a triangle.
How to calculate circumcircle radius?
To calculate circumcircle radius you should use the following formula:
circumcircle radius = (s * (s - a) * (s - b) * (s - c))^1/2 / s
